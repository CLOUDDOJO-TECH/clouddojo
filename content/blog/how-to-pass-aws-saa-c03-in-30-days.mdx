## Key Success Strategies

### 1. Active Learning, Not Passive Watching

âŒ **Don't**: Just watch videos without doing anything
âœ… **Do**: Pause videos and try concepts in AWS console

### 2. Practice Questions Are Critical

**Rule**: For every 1 hour of video, do 30 minutes of practice questions.

Why? Because:
- Questions teach you HOW the exam tests concepts
- You learn to eliminate wrong answers
- You identify gaps in knowledge faster

### 3. Learn Why Wrong Answers Are Wrong

Don't just memorize correct answers. Understand:
- Why is A correct?
- Why are B, C, D wrong?
- What makes them wrong?

CloudDojo's AI explanations are perfect for this!

### 4. Hands-On is Non-Negotiable

**You MUST use AWS console.** Reading about S3 vs actually creating buckets is completely different.

Spend at least 20-30% of study time in AWS.

### 5. Sleep & Health Matter

- Get 7-8 hours of sleep
- Exercise (even 20 minutes of walking helps memory)
- Eat healthy (brain food!)
- Stay hydrated

**Tired brain = Failed exam**
